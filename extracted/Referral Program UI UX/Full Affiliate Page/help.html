Getting Started

How do I create my first referral link?
Navigate to the "My Links" page and click the "Create Link" button. Enter a name for your link, the target URL, and optionally a custom slug. Your link will be generated instantly and ready to share.
What is the commission structure?
Commission rates vary by product tier. Bronze affiliates earn 5%, Silver earns 10%, Gold earns 15%, and Platinum earns 20% plus performance bonuses. Your tier is determined by your total conversion count.
How do I track my conversions?
All conversions are tracked automatically in real-time. Visit the "Conversions" page to see detailed information about each conversion, including customer details, product, amount, and your commission.
When do I get paid?
Payouts are processed on the 1st and 15th of each month. You must have a minimum balance of $50 to request a withdrawal. Processing takes 3-5 business days depending on your payout method.

Payments & Payouts

What payout methods are supported?
We support PayPal, Bank Transfer (ACH), and Cryptocurrency (USDC on Ethereum). You can add and manage multiple payout methods in the "Payouts" section of your dashboard.
Are there any payout fees?
PayPal charges a 1% fee, Crypto transfers have a 0.5% network fee, and Bank Transfers (ACH) are free. Express withdrawals (1-2 days) incur an additional $15 fee.
Why is my payout pending?
Payouts may be pending for several reasons: minimum balance not reached, account verification incomplete, or a hold period for new accounts. Contact support if your payout has been pending for more than 7 days.
Can I change my payout method?
Yes! Go to the "Payouts" page and click "Add Method" to add a new payout option. You can set any method as your default. Changes apply to the next scheduled payout.
